Step One

For Step One the shoes are shipped by the selling to one of our legit checking facilites.

Step Two

When the shoe arrives at the legit checking facilites it is checked over by several proffesionals and once they deem it legit they will repackage it and ship it to the buyer. If the shoes fail the legit check the buyer is issued a refund while the shoes are shipped back to the seller.

Step Three

The Shoes arrive at the buyers house for them to enjoy, if they are unhappy with how the shoes look or fit there is a seven day return policy where they can send back the shoes to the buyer and recieve a refund.
